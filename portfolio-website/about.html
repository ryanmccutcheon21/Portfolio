About me

Originally a baseball player my entire life, the sport took me to college at the University of Arkansas Fort-Smith, where I earned a Bachelor's of Science in Criminal Justice. After graduation, I met and married my wife, and soon we were expecting our first child. It was time to find a job. I ended up getting hired by the Fort Smith Fire Department. I have enjoyed my job, but my passion is designing and developing web and mobile apps. I am extremely motivated and self-taught. I love using my internal drive and passion, and applying it towards building the website, or app, of your dreams.
